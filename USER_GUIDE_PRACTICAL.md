## Getting Started

### Step 1: Visit SehaTalk

Open your web browser and go to: **https://sehatalk.com** (or your local instance)

### Step 2: Choose Your Role

On the homepage, you'll see two options:

| Option | For You If... |
|--------|---------------|
| **Join as Patient** | You have health questions or need medical guidance |
| **Join as Doctor** | You're a licensed medical professional |

### Step 3: Create Your Account

#### If You're a **Patient**:

1. Click "**Join as Patient**"
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Full Name** - Your first and last name
   - **Email** - A valid email address (for login & notifications)
   - **Phone** - Your phone number
   - **Date of Birth** - Your birth date
   - **Gender** - Select male or female
   - **Password** - Create a strong password
   - **Confirm Password** - Type the same password again
   - **Blood Type** (optional) - Useful for your medical profile
   - **Medical History** - List any chronic conditions
   - **Allergies** (optional) - Important for doctor consultations
   - **Current Medications** (optional) - Helps doctors understand your health

3. Accept the **Terms of Service**
4. Click "**Create Account**"
5. You'll be logged in automatically and can start using the platform

#### If You're a **Doctor**:

1. Click "**Join as Doctor**"
2. Complete the 4-step registration:

   **Step 1: Personal Information**
   - First and Last Name
   - Email (for login)
   - Phone number
   - Date of Birth
   - Gender

   **Step 2: Professional Information**
   - Medical Specialty (Cardiology, Dermatology, etc.)
   - Years of Experience
   - Professional Bio (tell patients about your background)

   **Step 3: Credentials**
   - Upload your medical license
   - Upload certificates or qualifications
   - These are reviewed by our admin team

   **Step 4: Secure Your Account**
   - Create a strong password
   - Confirm your password
   - Accept Terms of Service

3. Click "**Create Doctor Profile**"
4. Your account is created, but you'll need **verification** (see [Doctor Verification](#doctor-verification))

### Step 4: Complete Your Profile

After registration, complete your profile with:
- A **profile picture** (optional)
- Additional **medical history** (patients)
- **Professional experience** (doctors)

## For Patients

### Patient Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your **Patient Dashboard** with:

- **Your Profile** - View/edit your medical information
- **Questions Asked** - See all your previous questions
- **Bookmarks** - Save helpful discussions
- **Notifications** - Updates when doctors reply to your questions

### Asking a Medical Question

#### How to Ask:

1. Click "**Ask a Question**" (prominent button in navigation)
2. Fill in the question form:

   | Field | What to Do | Example |
   |-------|-----------|---------|
   | **Question Title** | Be specific and clear | "What causes frequent headaches?" |
   | **Detailed Description** | Explain your symptoms, duration, previous treatments | "I've had severe headaches for 2 weeks, worse in the morning..." |
   | **Medical Category** | Choose the relevant health topic | Neurology, General Practice, etc. |
   | **Attach Files** (optional) | Add medical test results, images | PDF of lab reports, X-ray images |

3. Review your question for clarity
4. Click "**Submit Question**"

#### Tips for Better Answers:

- ‚úì Be specific about symptoms (when, where, how severe)
- ‚úì Mention any allergies or current medications
- ‚úì Include relevant medical history
- ‚úì Provide context (recent travel, stress, lifestyle changes)
- ‚úì Ask follow-up questions clearly

#### Your Privacy:

- Your medical information is **private and secure**
- You can ask anonymously (your name doesn't appear)
- Only verified doctors can see your medical history details
- You control who can contact you

### Browsing Questions

#### Explore Tab:

1. Click "**Explore**" to see all public questions
2. Use filters:
   - **Search** - Find specific health topics
   - **Category** - Browse by medical specialty
   - **Sort** - Newest, Most Popular, or Trending

3. Click on any question to see:
   - The original question
   - All doctor replies
   - Doctor's profile and credentials
   - Patient comments and discussions

#### Understanding the Display:

| Element | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| **‚úì Verified Badge** | Doctor is confirmed and licensed |
| **üëÅÔ∏è View Count** | How many people read this question |
| **üëç Upvote Count** | How many found the answer helpful |
| **üí¨ Reply Count** | How many doctors responded |

### Interacting with Answers

#### Upvoting Answers:

1. Read a doctor's response
2. Click the **üëç Upvote** button if you found it helpful
3. Your upvote is counted and helps others find good answers
4. You can only upvote once per question (logged-in users only)

#### Reading Doctor Profiles:

1. Click on a doctor's name or photo in an answer
2. See their:
   - Specialization
   - Years of experience
   - Professional bio
   - Number of patients helped
   - Verification status
   - Rating/reputation

### Your Questions & Privacy

#### Editing Your Question:

1. Go to your Patient Dashboard
2. Find your question in "**My Questions**"
3. Click the **Edit** button
4. Modify your question
5. Click **Save**

#### Deleting Your Question:

1. Go to "**My Questions**"
2. Click the **Delete** button
3. Confirm deletion
4. Your question and all responses are removed

#### Making Your Question Private:

- Mark questions as "**Private**" so only you and replied doctors see them
- Default is **Public** (visible to all users)

## For Doctors

### Doctor Dashboard

After verification, your Doctor Dashboard includes:

- **Your Profile** - Professional information and bio
- **Questions to Answer** - Pending questions in your specialty
- **Your Replies** - Answers you've given
- **Statistics** - Patients helped, reply count, rating
- **Notifications** - New questions in your specialty

### Getting Verified

#### Why Verification Matters:

- ‚úì Patients see the **verified badge** next to your name
- ‚úì Your credentials are confirmed
- ‚úì You build trust with patients
- ‚úì Access to all platform features
- ‚úì Included in "Expert Doctors" listings

#### Verification Process:

1. **Upload Credentials** during signup:
   - Medical license/diploma
   - Qualification certificates
   - Professional ID (optional)

2. **Admin Review** (24-48 hours):
   - Our team reviews your documents
   - You'll receive an email confirmation

3. **Verification Status**:
   - Verified ‚úì - Your credentials are confirmed
   - Pending ‚è≥ - Under review
   - Rejected ‚úó - Resubmit valid credentials

4. **Once Verified**:
   - Badge appears on your profile
   - Access to answer questions
   - Listed in doctor directory

#### Need Help with Verification?

Contact support with your details and we'll help expedite the process.

### Answering Patient Questions

#### Finding Questions:

1. Go to "**Pending Questions**" in your dashboard
2. Filter by your specialty
3. Browse available questions

#### Answering a Question:

1. Click on a question you want to answer
2. Review the patient's:
   - Symptoms and medical history
   - Previous answers (if any)
   - Specific concerns

3. Click "**Add Your Response**"
4. Type your medical guidance:
   - Keep language simple for patients
   - Be thorough but concise
   - Avoid overly technical terms
   - Suggest when in-person consultation is needed

5. Click "**Post Reply**"

#### Important Guidelines:

- ‚úì Provide evidence-based medical information
- ‚úì Disclose any conflicts of interest
- ‚úì Recommend professional consultation for serious cases
- ‚úì Be empathetic and respectful
- ‚úì Don't make definitive diagnoses online
- ‚úó Don't share patient information
- ‚úó Don't advertise your practice
- ‚úó Don't be disrespectful or dismissive

### Building Your Reputation

#### Earn Trust Through:

1. **Quality Answers**
   - Detailed, helpful responses
   - Evidence-based information
   - Follow-up to patient questions

2. **Consistency**
   - Answer regularly
   - Respond promptly
   - Build a portfolio of good answers

3. **Patient Ratings**
   - Patients upvote helpful answers
   - Your rating appears on your profile
   - Higher ratings = more visibility

#### Your Professional Profile

Your profile shows:
- **Name & Specialty** - What patients search for
- **Verification Badge** - Trust indicator
- **Professional Bio** - Your background

- **Statistics** - Questions answered, patient count

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is SehaTalk free?**  
A: Yes, completely free for all users. No hidden fees or premium upgrades.

**Q: What languages are supported?**  
A: Arabic (default) and English. Switch anytime with the language toggle.

**Q: Do I need to be verified to ask questions?**  
A: No, any registered patient can ask questions. Doctors need verification to answer.

**Q: Is my information really private?**  
A: Yes. Your medical information is encrypted and only visible to doctors. Read our [Privacy Policy](#privacy--security).

**Q: Can I remain anonymous?**  
A: Patients can ask without revealing their name. Information is still secure.

### Patient Questions

**Q: How long does it take to get an answer?**  
A: Most questions get responses within 24 hours. Verified doctors actively monitor their specialties.

**Q: Can I ask the same question to multiple doctors?**  
A: Yes. Different doctors may provide varied perspectives. You can ask follow-ups.

**Q: What if I don't like an answer?**  
A: You can ask for clarification, get a second opinion from another doctor, or report if inappropriate.

**Q: Can doctors see my real name and contact info?**  
A: Only if you choose to include it. By default, only your first name appears.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**  
A: Go to **Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Delete Account**. This removes all your data permanently.

### Doctor Questions

**Q: How long does verification take?**  
A: Usually 24-48 hours. Check your email for status updates.

**Q: What documents do I need to verify?**  
A: Medical license/diploma and relevant qualifications. Scans are acceptable.

**Q: Can I advertise my clinic?**  
A: No, advertising is not permitted. Focus on helping patients with quality answers.

**Q: Do I get paid for answers?**  
A: No, this is a volunteer platform. You get reputation and exposure instead.

**Q: Can I see patient contact information?**  
A: Only if the patient shares it. Never contact patients outside the platform.

**Q: What if a patient asks something outside my specialty?**  
A: Skip it. Recommend they ask in a different category. Only answer in your specialty.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers work best?**  
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Latest versions recommended.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**  
A: Yes, fully mobile-responsive. Works on iOS and Android devices.

**Q: Why am I getting logged out?**  
A: Session expires after inactivity. Log back in to continue.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**  
A: Go to **Settings ‚Üí Account** and update email. You'll need to verify the new address.

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "Invalid email or password" | Check spelling, reset password if forgotten |
| "Email not found" | Create account first at /auth |
| "Account locked" | Multiple failed logins lock account. Wait 30 minutes or reset password |
| "Verification email not received" | Check spam folder, request new email |

### Account Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "Can't upload documents" | Check file size (max 10MB), format (PDF, JPG, PNG) |
| "Doctor verification pending" | Check email for updates, contact support if delayed |
| "Can't ask questions" | Ensure you're logged in as patient |
| "Can't answer questions" | You must be verified doctor first |

### Content Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "Question won't submit" | Fill all required fields, check character count |
| "Can't see doctor's answer" | Refresh page, check if question still exists |
| "Images not loading" | Clear browser cache, try different browser |
| "Text looks misaligned" | Try different language, reload page |

### Performance Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "Slow loading" | Check internet connection, clear browser cache |
| "Page freezes" | Close other browser tabs, restart browser |
| "Notifications not working" | Check notification permissions in browser settings |

### Report Issues

For unresolved problems:
1. Email: **support@sehatalk.com**
2. Include: Problem description, screenshot, device/browser info
3. Response time: 24-48 hours
